Delicious Cheesecake

The creamiest dessert you'll ever enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 2 tbsp of sugar (for the crust)
  • 4 packs (8 oz each) of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1/4 cup of heavy cream

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and 2 tbsp sugar. Press firmly into the bottom of a springform pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add vanilla, then mix in eggs one at a time. Blend in sour cream and heavy cream.
  5. Pour the batter over the crust.
  6. Bake for 55–60 minutes until the center is just slightly jiggly.
  7. Turn off the oven, crack the oven door, and let the cheesecake rest inside for 1 hour (prevents cracks).
  8. Refrigerate at least 4 hours (best overnight) before serving.

Notes

  • Add lemon zest to the batter for a refreshing twist!
  • For a rich topping, spread strawberry or blueberry compote before serving.
  • A water bath (baking pan with hot water under the cheesecake) helps prevent cracks.
  • If you want a bakery-style look, whip some cream and pipe swirls around the edges before adding fresh fruit.
